📟 On-Page SEO Audits & Reports

The On-page Auditor can do an extensive on-page SEO analysis for one or more pages at a time. The onpage_audit action will return an extensive audit report with analysis of: titles, meta descriptions, alt tags, structured data, open graph elements, aria-labels, keywords, and more.

It is also equipped with a fetch_links action that can retrieve all internal and outgoing hyperlinks from a page and provide on-page link metrics. The fetch_links action can also be used in one prompt together with the onpage_audit action.

🔎 Keyword Analyses

The foundation of every successful SEO strategy. The keyword_analysis action from Pro SEO Assistant GPT goes far beyond listing keywords. It integrates search volumes, competition data, and user intent signals to highlight the best ranking opportunities. 🏷️ Optimize Meta Tags & Alt Tags

Maximize Your SEO Impact with Optimized Tags. Meta tags and alt tags significantly influence how search engines interpret your content and how users perceive your site in search results. Pro SEO Assistant GPT thoroughly reviews and optimizes your site's title tags, meta descriptions, and alt tags, ensuring they effectively target your primary keywords, clearly communicate your content’s relevance, and enhance accessibility.

Optimizing these elements can dramatically increase your pages' click-through rates (CTR) by crafting compelling meta descriptions and title tags that attract users’ attention in search engine results pages (SERPs). Properly structured alt tags also help search engines understand images on your pages, providing additional context that can further boost your visibility and improve accessibility for visually impaired users.

Key Optimizations Provided:

  • ✅ Title Tags: Precise, keyword-rich titles optimized for relevance and length.
  • ✅ Meta Descriptions: Engaging, concise descriptions designed to increase user engagement and CTR.
  • ✅ Image Alt Tags: Descriptive and accurate alt texts that enhance SEO value and accessibility.
  • ✅ Consistency Checks: Ensures consistency of messaging across all tags and on-page elements.
  • ✅ Best Practices Guidance: Actionable insights to maintain tag quality over time.
